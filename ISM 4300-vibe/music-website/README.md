
# SongScape Website


A simple, modern music-themed website called SongScape with two main pages:

- **Home:** Displays the top 10 hits of today.
- **Music Quiz:** A quiz that recommends a song based on your mood and genre preference.

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your browser to view the Home page with the top 10 hits.
2. Use the navigation bar to go to the Music Quiz page (`quiz.html`).
3. Take the quiz and get a music recommendation based on your answers.

## Project Structure

- `index.html` — Home page (Top 10 Hits)
- `quiz.html` — Music Recommendation Quiz
- `style.css` — Modern music-themed styles
- `top10.js` — Loads the top 10 hits
- `quiz.js` — Handles quiz logic and recommendations

## Customization
- You can update the top 10 hits in `top10.js`.
- You can add more moods, genres, or recommendations in `quiz.js`.
